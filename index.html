about: Just by looking at what someone is being advertised can tell a lot about them. For my Digital Shadow project I chose to compile all the ads I got for 3 days into a collection of images to showcase a bit about me. By focusing on advertising that I typically skip and have become accustomed to ignoring I saw how just from looking at what advertising was being targetted to me digitally you could get a sense of so many random things about me. By compiling the advertisements I run into I can create a portrait of how my digital identity looks from a commercial perspective. These images are presented through popups.
